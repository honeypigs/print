公司简介

深圳创宏智控技术有限公司是由博士、博士后和硕士研究生为技术骨干的年轻创业团队于2017年成立的高科技企业。

公司致力于建设智能三维成型数字化生态系统，业务领域涉及3D打印机的研发、制造、销售和服务，3D打印技术解决方案、3D打印教育培训和3D打印服务等。

目前在3D建模软件、3D打印切片软件、STEAM教育与3D数字化技术融合教育方案、机器人图形化编程软件等方面积累了丰富的技术开发及应用经验，并与哈尔滨工业大学、清华大学等高校形成深入的产学研合作关系。

公司以“客户至上、技术争先、团结协作”为企业宗旨，秉承“专注技术、服务客户、求真务实”的企业文化，牢记“精于技术、质量为本、贴心服务”的经营理念，立足客户需求的创新，努力成长为3D打印行业创新技术领导者。

Company profile

Shenzhen TronHoo Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise established in 2017 by a young entrepreneurial team with doctoral, postdoctoral and master as technical backbone.

The company is committed to building an intelligent three-dimensional molding digital ecosystem, and its business areas include research and development, manufacturing, sales and services of 3D printers, 3D printing technology solutions, 3D printing education and training, and 3D printing services.

At present, he has accumulated rich experience in technology development and application in 3D modeling software, slicing software, STEAM education and 3D digital technology integration education scheme, robot graphical programming software. And has formed in-depth industry-university-research cooperation relationship with Harbin Institute of Technology, Tsinghua University and other universities.

The company takes "customer first, best in technology, unity and cooperation" as its corporate tenet, adhering to the corporate culture of "focusing on technology, serving customers, seeking truth and being practical", keeping in mind the business philosophy of "being skilled in technology, quality first, caring service", and based on the innovation of customer needs, it strives to grow into an innovative technology leader in the 3D printing industry.\
